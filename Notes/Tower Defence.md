
- Tower types
	- [[Packet pad]]
		-  Sends [[packets]] to locations. 
	- [[Turrets]]
		- requires [[packets]] to operate

Theme
Junkyard
- lost of scrap, turrets made of old parts.
- packet launchers are mouse traps
- Rats for enemies and allies.
- springs and stuff


Gameplay
Transfers [[packets]] to [[turrets]] by connecting [[packet pad]]s to each other.
Levels could have blockers (walls to block [[packets]])
Player must plan around this and optimise packet delivery
